Who are we ?

ZTRIC was founded by three US based Indian engineers and a seasoned renewable energy investor. We returned to India to give back to India and help India achieve energy independence and reduce our carbon footprint. We created a business to especially serve those discerning communities who value long term relationships, expect the best, but with little or no capital access.

We are a ‘Distributed Green Electricity’ company. We own and operate, premium engineered rooftop solar electricity systems. We are a wholly owned subsidiary of Ztric, Inc. based in Austin, TX. Our founders and investors have over 15 years of experience running energy and related companies in the United States.

WHY ARE WE DOING THIS ?

ZTRIC was founded by engineers who care about the environment and about India. All current solar electricity providers are doing it for the incentives, government subsidies and tax breaks. They are also currently servicing only larger commercial consumers.

At ZTRIC, we believe that unless we are on every rooftop in India, we cannot make any significant impact. Hence our target market is smaller consumers such as yourself who have been currently left out of the market with no real options to go solar. You should choose ZTRIC if you are looking for a hassle free, zero upfront cost solar solution with savings on your energy costs, while going green.

As 13 of the top 20 most polluted cities in the world are in India, we believe this is an important step in reducing our carbon footprint and pollution levels.

Zero-Upfront Cost

We invest and install our solar electricity platform on your roof. You use it just like regular electricity and pay per unit produced.

Affordable

Our PPA model allows you to buy electricity from us just like you do today from your utility company. Our rates are typically 10% - 20% cheaper.

Hassle-Free

You enjoy clean green electricity while we do the rest. Warranty and maintenance is included for the term of the contract.

Environment Friendly

By going green, you are helping our planet. You can earning 'green karma' and helping leave a better planet for your future generations.
